## 📋 What is TaviNote?

TaviNote is an all-in-one travel planning app. It lets you organize your entire trip in one place. You can:

- Create your trip schedule with the itinerary tool.
- Write trip memories in the diary section.
- Manage your packing list to avoid forgetting items.
- Track your spending with the budget manager.

It works offline and online, so you can access your information anytime. TaviNote uses simple menus and buttons. Anyone can plan a trip without stress.
